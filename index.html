Research interest

I am broadly interested in research on Reinforcement Learning, and my current works focus on the generalization and adaptation of general agents. I aim to develop the principled algorithms with

  • high degree of learning efficiency by leveraging diverse modals of prior knowledge (e.g., demonstrations, offline data, human-in-the-loop, skills, etc).
  • generalization and robustness concerning out-of-distribution tasks and unexpected perturbations.
  • interpretability and simplicity
